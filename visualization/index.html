Rejection Reasons

  • Buffer Full – No space in the queue when a job arrives.
  • Timeout – Job exceeds its wait tolerance.
  • Machine Fail – Job is scrapped during a machine breakdown (25% chance).
  • Quality Reject – Job fails final quality inspection.

Key Formulas

  • Complexity = Base Complexity + Quality Risk (Beta(2,2) scaled to 0–2)
  • Priority Level = 0.6 × Weight + 0.4 × (Complexity / 7 × 5)
  • Safe Wait Mean = max(0.5, 10 / (1 + Priority Level)) → higher priority = shorter tolerance
  • Quality Scrap Prob = min(0.20, 0.02 × Complexity)
  • Time‑to‑Failure = Exponential(mean 120 h)  |  Repair Time = Uniform(1, 4) h

Inspection time follows Gamma(mean 0.25 h, sd 0.08 h) truncated at 0.05 h.
